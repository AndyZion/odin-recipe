Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is the most popular italian traditional dish.

As it mainly contains 3 main ingredients and easy to cook, it was served to the working class.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup all purpose flour
  • 100 ml tomato ketchup
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 teaspoon virgin olive oil
  • 200 mg mozzarella
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dry yeast
  • water as required

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Prepare the base
  3. Spread the tomato sauce
  4. Spead the mozzarella over the sauce
  5. Bake at 200'C for 10-15 minutes
